Blox Fruits Leveling Guide :)

Level 1 - 50

Location: Starter Island

Enemies: Bandits, Trainee Pirates

Tip: Focus on melee combat or a beginner fruit like Chop or Bomb.

Level 51 - 150

Location: Pirate Village

Enemies: Pirates, Brutes

Tip: Start investing in better fruits like Flame or Ice for faster grinding.

Level 151 - 300

Location: Desert Island, Frozen Village

Enemies: Desert Bandits, Snow Bandits

Tip: Unlock new abilities and consider getting the Light fruit for better mobility.

Level 301 - 700

Location: Skylands, Fountain City

Enemies: Sky Pirates, Galley Pirates

Tip: Use powerful AoE (Area of Effect) fruits like Magma or Light for quicker farming.

Level 701 - Max Level

Location: Second Sea, Third Sea

Enemies: Advanced NPCs and Bosses

Tip: Get awakened fruits and team up with friends to defeat bosses faster.

All Blox Fruits and Their Info

Fruit Name Type Description
Bomb Natural Good for beginners, basic explosive attacks.
Chop Natural Grants immunity to sword attacks.
Flame Elemental Fire-based abilities with good AoE.
Ice Elemental Freezes enemies and provides mobility on water.
Light Elemental Fastest flight in the game, great for grinding.
Magma Elemental High damage with lava-based attacks.
Dark Elemental Darkness-based abilities with crowd control.
Dragon Beast Transforms the user into a dragon with powerful attacks.
Dough Natural Unique moves with sticky and powerful abilities.
Gravity Natural Controls gravity to deal damage and control the battlefield.
